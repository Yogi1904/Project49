Some interesting facts about chameleons:

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part if the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Some interesting facts about teddy bears:

  • Teddy bears are stuffed toys in the form of bears, named after the American President Theodore Roosevelt.

Some interesting facts about pandas:

  • Pandas are a native to South Central China.
  • Giant pandas spend 10-16 hours a day feeding, mainly on bamboo.
  • 26 zoos in 20 countries house giant pandas. But they are found in the wild only in China.

Some interesting facts about camels:

  • Camels are known as the ships of the deserts.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • They have humps in their back, where they store food for the long desert trecks.
